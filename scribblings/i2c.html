i2c: integer -> char

Salt Ho

 #lang i2c package: i2c

1 Introduction

This is a domain-specific language that relies on brag language. You can translate the integer into char like

#lang i2c
72 101 108 108 111 44
32 119 111 114 108 100 33

We will get
Hello, world!
